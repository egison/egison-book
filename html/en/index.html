Egison Book

About this book

Egison is a programming language designed to make pattern matching and mathematical notation first-class tools. This book introduces the language from its foundations and develops practical techniques for symbolic and tensor computation.

What you will learn

  1. 01

    Pattern matching

    Describe patterns directly, even for sets, multisets, graphs, and other non-free data types.

  2. 02

    Computer algebra

    Work with symbolic expressions, simplification rules, and extensible mathematical functions.

  3. 03

    Tensor notation

    Express tensor calculations and differential geometry with index notation.
